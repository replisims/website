- **DGM parameters**

    * Variable
- **Implementation of methods**
- **Simulation structure**
    * Pseudocode
    * Flow-chart
    * Data dependence
- **“Replicator degrees of freedom”**
    * Ambiguous information
    * Sources consulted
        + referenced articles
